9 Steps from Diagnosis to Scale

Every SaaS company has one dominant constraint holding it back. This framework systematically finds it, designs the fix, and builds the engine that compounds.

01
Context Mapping
Map company stage, revenue motion, ICP maturity, capital pressure, competitive density, and organisational execution capacity before suggesting any action.
Stage AnalysisICP MaturityCapital Pressure

Dominant Constraint Diagnosis
Decompose the business into demand generation, conversion efficiency, value delivery, retention depth, monetization capture, and scalability. Find the single highest-leverage bottleneck.
Bottleneck IDCascading Impact
03
Revenue Architecture Design
Map acquisition engines, activation triggers, pricing mechanics, expansion pathways, and retention loops as one interconnected system. Design compounding flywheels.
Flywheel DesignCAC Efficiency
04
Unit Economics Governance
Evaluate CAC realism, LTV expansion headroom, margin sustainability, discount dependency risk, and burn productivity across conservative, moderate, and aggressive scenarios.

Adoption Behaviour Analysis
Diagnose trust barriers, cognitive load, onboarding friction, and perceived ROI clarity. Design behavioural nudges and usage reinforcement loops.

Network EffectsSwitching Costs
